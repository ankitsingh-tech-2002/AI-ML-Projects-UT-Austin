# Austo Automobile Market Analysis

**Domain:** Exploratory Data Analysis / Customer Segmentation
**Program:** The University of Texas at Austin AI & ML Post Graduate Program

## Objective

Analyze customer demographics, financial attributes, and purchasing behavior to understand car-buying trends in the US automobile market and help Austo identify potential customer segments and market opportunities.

## Key Focus Areas

* Customer profiling and segmentation
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Consumer purchase behavior analysis
* Demographic and income-based trend analysis
* Business recommendations for market expansion

## Project Workflow

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Univariate and bivariate analysis
* Customer demographic analysis
* Income and loan pattern analysis
* Car preference analysis across customer segments
* Business insight generation and recommendations

## How to Run

1. Open the project notebook in Jupyter Notebook or Google Colab.
2. Ensure `austo_automobile.csv` is placed in the same directory as the notebook.
3. Install required dependencies:

   * Pandas
   * NumPy
   * Matplotlib
   * Seaborn
4. Run all notebook cells sequentially to reproduce the analysis and visualizations.

## Dataset Features

The dataset includes customer demographic, financial, and purchasing information such as:

* Age and Gender
* Profession and Education
* Marital Status and Dependents
* Salary and Household Income
* Personal and House Loan Status
* Partner Employment Information
* Preferred Car Type (Hatchback, Sedan, SUV)
* Car Price

## Business Impact

The analysis helps Austo better understand customer preferences and purchasing behavior in the US automobile market. The generated insights support data-driven decision-making for product positioning, target customer identification, marketing strategy, and future production planning.
